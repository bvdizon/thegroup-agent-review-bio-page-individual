About Will Vo

Will Vo knows real estate in Calgary. Born and raised here, Will is here to help you find your perfect home. He’ll take the time to really listen to what you are wanting in your new home, and then he’ll use his knowledge of Calgary gained from a lifetime of living here to locate the houses, townhouses or condos on the market that best fit your needs, wants and budget. Once you find that perfect home, Will Vo will be YOUR advocate with the seller. He’s passionate about his work and is results-oriented. He gets the job done.

If you’re thinking of selling your current home, you can count on Will to tell you the truth about what your home will realistically sell for on the market today. And, as YOUR Calgary REALTOR®, he’ll successfully present your home and make it stand out from the crowd in today’s Calgary real estate market. Having earned a Marketing Diploma from SAIT, Will’s got the formal training plus years of practical, hands-on experience in marketing, sales, advertising and the effective use of social media platforms to sell your home for the best possible price in the shortest possible time.

Why choose Will to represent you? He’s driven. Hardworking. Talented. Tough when necessary. Honest. Detail oriented. Will’s also been on your side of the table. Before becoming a REALTOR®, he worked over seven years in the oil and gas industry in Calgary. He bought, sold and rented out properties himself. He understands what it’s like to be the client, what a client needs from their REALTOR®, and how a client wants to be treated by their REALTOR®. Will Vo truly is Calgary’s REALTOR®.
